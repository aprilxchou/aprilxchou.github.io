Featured Projects
Craniofacial Segmentation Training Dataset
Annotated 400+ CBCT scans of craniofacial structures — including the cranium, orbits, maxilla, mandible, nasal passages, and sinuses — using 3D Slicer's Segment Editor. Scans were exported as structured NIFTI files and used as ground-truth training data for a deep learning model targeting automated orthodontic treatment planning. Developed consistent labeling protocols and performed cross-validation review to ensure dataset quality.

Cephalometric Landmark Identification & QA
Developed a quality assurance protocol for cephalometric landmark annotation on lateral skull radiographs, covering key points including Sella (S), Nasion (N), A-point, B-point, and Pogonion. Established inter-rater reliability metrics across a team of 6 annotators, reducing landmark placement error and improving consistency of AI training data for automated cephalometric analysis tools.

Ongoing QA Research
Biostatistical Analysis of Orthodontic Treatment Outcomes
Applied linear regression and mixed-effects models to orthodontic patient data to identify predictors of treatment duration and success. Key predictors included ANB angle, SNA, and Frankfort-Mandibular plane angle. Analysis stratified patients by skeletal classification (Class I, II, III). Results visualized in R using ggplot2 and shared with the clinical research team at UNC.
